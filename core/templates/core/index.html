Waste Reduction Initiative

EcoKochi is leading the way in implementing innovative waste reduction strategies across the city:

  • Source Reduction Programs targeting households and businesses
  • Smart Procurement Guidelines for government and corporate sectors
  • Reusable Container Incentives for local food vendors and markets
  • Single-Use Plastic Alternatives available at subsidized rates
  • Product Lifetime Extension through repair workshops and skills training

Our approach has already reduced municipal waste by 23% in pilot neighborhoods, with plans to expand citywide by 2026.

Recycling & Composting Programs

Our comprehensive recycling and composting initiatives are transforming Kochi's waste management landscape:

  • Neighborhood Recycling Centers with 24/7 access
  • Community Composting Hubs for organic waste processing
  • Material Recovery Facilities with advanced sorting technology
  • Door-to-Door Collection for separated recyclables
  • School Composting Programs teaching sustainable practices

These efforts have diverted over 65% of waste from landfills in participating communities, creating valuable resources from what was once considered trash.

Environmental Education

EcoKochi believes education is the foundation for lasting environmental change:

  • School Curriculum Enhancement with waste management modules
  • Public Workshops on sustainable living practices
  • Business Training Programs for corporate sustainability
  • Digital Learning Platforms accessible to all citizens
  • Community Ambassadors Program training local environmental leaders

Our education initiatives have reached over 65,000 students and 25,000 adults across Kochi, building a community of environmentally conscious citizens.

Waste Reporting

Our mobile app makes it easy to report waste issues in your community:

  • Snap a photo of illegal dumping, overflowing bins, or other waste issues
  • GPS-tagging for accurate location reporting
  • AI-powered waste classification to help prioritize response
  • Real-time status updates as your report is processed
  • Direct communication with waste management authorities

Since launching this feature, response times to waste issues have decreased by 76%, with over 5,000 successful cleanups initiated through citizen reporting.

Real-Time Tracking

Stay informed with our advanced real-time tracking features:

  • Live Collection Schedule updates with ETA for your neighborhood
  • Smart Bin Monitoring showing capacity levels across the city
  • Route Optimization to reduce collection inefficiencies
  • Personalized Waste Calendar with reminder notifications
  • Historical Data Analysis to track your neighborhood's performance

This transparency has improved collection efficiency by 42% and reduced fuel consumption by 28% across our waste management fleet.

Community Rewards

About EcoKochi

EcoKochi is a pioneering smart city initiative focused on revolutionizing waste management in Kochi through technology, community engagement, and sustainable practices.

Our mission is to create a cleaner, greener Kochi by empowering citizens, leveraging IoT technology, and implementing innovative waste management solutions.

Key Objectives:

  • Reduce waste generation by 30% by 2028
  • Achieve 80% waste diversion from landfills
  • Create a transparent waste management ecosystem
  • Engage 75% of citizens in active participation
  • Establish Kochi as a model for sustainable urban living
